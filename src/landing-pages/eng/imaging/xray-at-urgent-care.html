  • X-RAY
    An X-ray is a type of imaging test that uses high-energy waves to produce pictures of the inside of your body. They are a type of electromagnetic radiation.

  • THE OLDEST MEDICAL IMAGING
    X-rays are the oldest and most frequently used form of medical imaging.

What are COVID-19 symptom?

Symptom include fatigue, shortness of breath, chest pain, and problems with concentration and memory. In some cases, long COVID can also lead to depression and anxiety.


Can people with mild COVID-19 symptom recover at home?

Yes. Most people with mild COVID-19 symptoms can recover at home. You can treat the symptoms with over-the-counter medications, such as Tylenol, Advil, and Motrin.


What is asymptomatic transmission?

Asymptomatic transmission is the spread of a disease from an infected individual who does not display any symptoms.
This can occur when the individual is in the incubation period of the disease,
which is the time between exposure and when symptoms first appear.
